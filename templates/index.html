DYNAMICALLY GENERATED PLAYLIST
CONCEPT

The core concept behind this application revolves around a dynamic playlist generation. Unlike conventional playlists, where songs are pre-defined, my approach adapts to user preferences during playback. The songs following the currently visible ones in the playlist are swapped based on the user's listening habits. This algorithm ensures that the playlist aligns closely with the user's preferences and enhances their overall music experience.


USAGE
To begin, select the type of song by clicking on the corresponding categories: A, B, C and so on [section 1]. This initiates the playlist generation process. Once you've made your initial selection, click on any category within the playlist [section 3] and simply scroll downwards to observe the effects.


NOTE
The choice of categories and shuffling of songs in the playlist is accentuated with the intention of showcasing the functionality of the dynamic playlist concept

×

SONG TYPE SELECTION

When selecting songs based on specific genres, playlist generator offers a straightforward process. Similar to the initial song choice, the generator compiles songs from the chosen category. What sets it apart is its ability to enrich your playlist by incorporating songs from related genres. These related tracks, sharing thematic similarities, seamlessly integrate into the playlist.
